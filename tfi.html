Multiplying by 25

25 = 100 ÷ 4

Then, if we want to multiply a number by 25, we can divide that number by 4 then multiply it by 100.

Example: 12 × 25

12 × 25 = 12 × (100 ÷ 4) = (12 ÷ 4) × 100 = 3 × 100 = 300

12 × 25 = 300

Example: 25 × 61

25 × 61 = (100 ÷ 4) × 61 = 100 × (61 ÷ 4) = 100 × 15.25 = 1525

25 × 61 = 1525
